Prabhas

An Indian actor who works in Telugu, Hindi, and Tamil films.

Prabhas, born Venkata Satyanarayana Prabhas Raju Uppalapati, is an Indian actor known for his work in Telugu, Hindi, and Tamil cinema. He gained nationwide popularity with his role as Baahubali in the Baahubali series, which is one of the highest-grossing Indian films of all time.

Prabhas has received several awards and nominations for his performances. He is also known for his dedication to his roles, often undergoing rigorous physical transformations for his characters.

"I believe the script is the real hero of a film. And I have always been careful while selecting scripts."
